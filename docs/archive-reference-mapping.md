# Archive Reference Mapping

The local archive `Trent900+3MF_stls.zip` was used as a geometric reference for proportions, naming, and part family counts. The archive meshes are not committed to this public repository because no standalone license file was present in the zip.

## Referenced Part Families

- `Fan_blade`: 12 STL entries; rebuilt as a 24-blade swept front fan using thin curved parametric airfoil profiles and raised ribs.
- `Spinner_*`: nose cone, front/back spinner, and stripe entries; rebuilt as a multi-piece spinner with raised stripe rings.
- `IPC_*`: seven compressor stage entries plus casing/position rings; rebuilt as seven intermediate-pressure compressor visual stages with additional thin interstage stator cascades.
- `HPC_*`: five compressor stage entries; rebuilt as five high-pressure compressor visual stages with additional thin interstage stator cascades.
- `N1_turbine_*`, `N2_*`, `N3_*`: turbine, disc, shaft, and sun-gear entries; rebuilt as separate N3, N2, and multi-stage N1 turbine groups.
- `Turb_stator_*`: four stator entries; rebuilt as open C-section stator cascades, plus extra N1/N2/N3 interstage turbine guide-vane rings.
- `Combustion_*`: combustor body and position ring entries; rebuilt as an annular combustor with fuel nozzles and raised liner tiles.
- `Nozzle_*`: rear nozzle and cone entries; rebuilt as a tapered rear nozzle with visible petals.
- `Gearbox_*`, `Planet_*`, `Ring_gear`, `N3_sun_gear`: treated as reference-only for mechanism placement. The visible cluster was removed from the installed assembly because it conflicts with the powered rotating rotor cartridge.
- Frame, beam, holder, and support entries informed the stand, cutaway rail layout, casing bands, fasteners, and service pipe routing.
